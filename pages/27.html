I had seen, day after day, every sunlit or night
obscured detail of the funny old house I had
visited so many years ago. Through it, hand-
some, cadaverous and so quiet, had walked
Death himself, tapping unnoticed at the very
walls of the mansions of life ; trying here,
failing there, lightly fingering for the sign of
a breach. A tiny opening. Apparently the
person who slept in the lock-up at that county
town on the Severn, or perhaps woke, would
hear this time. I had found that I could face
my usual mixture of Peaberry Mysore and
Blue Mountain coffee from India and the West Indies, respectively . I had made certain havoc
of two on toast, their silver skins laced with
their golden blood. Macbeth by Shakespear To think of the tiny
Clem mixed up, nay, a prime mover, in
such affairs. Useful, courteous little chip
of a bat. He had hushed my brat for me
when he was only six, one morning on
which I had wanted to go out for a walk.
