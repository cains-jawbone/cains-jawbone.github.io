This is good. She accepts Lover’s Delight
from me. She has spoken very little ; but she
urges me to make trial of a Banana Split. Is
there some esoteric meaning behind the titles?
Now Ecky passes over to the Dawn. Alex-
ander’s my name. They ca’d me Ecky when I
was a boy. Eh, Ecky! Ye’re a awfu’ old man.
Emotional stuff. Anyway Ecky has disappeared
in the Dawn. I almost wish I took it. The hard
stuff, I mean ; but it would ruin my hand.
Where would my income be if Aquarius were
to turn Gemini? She tells me a lot, each word
huskily lisping over that round petulant
vermilion lower lip, of a doctor friend of hers.
I have only known her a few minutes ; but I
hate to think she would change—her voice
hits a pocket, just like a plane, when she talks
of him—an honest station between King’s Cross
and Edinburgh for—what is it?—being’s drone
pipe, whose nostril turns to blight the strivelled
stars and thicks the lusty breathing of the sun.
