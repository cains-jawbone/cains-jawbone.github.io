John and Cornelius, the Dort people The murder of the De Witts, on the 10th (or 20th?) of August 1672, was an atrocity which attracted much attention throughout Europe. John and Cornelius de Witt, born at Dort, in Holland, were the sons of a burgomaster of that town.
