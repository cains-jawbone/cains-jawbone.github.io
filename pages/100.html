I dimly guess why the old dead so wanted this.
I had worked for him, Henry had worked for
him. If I could get up, as, believe me, I cannot,
I would have a thing to say to her. She lolls
over at me gloating, her mouth blood-tinted
on the puma freckle of her beauty. Why
should I think of Henry at this particular
juncture? I have it. Scotland Yard, of course.
And little ’twill matter to one. A sorry thing
to be last noticed : the buttonhole has escaped
from the buttonholer. He, the reckless old cock,
slips down past Woolworth’s and she continues
full-sail toward the Kursaal, as flush—oh, you
wicked woman—as May. The girl is smiling
at me. That’s not so good. Here I shake off
the bur o’ the world, man’s congregation shun.
O beastly woman. You know not how ill’s all
here, about my heart ; but I know. Henry,
I feel it, is for the first and last time getting out
of hand. Good-bye, Henry. He drops awa. . . .
