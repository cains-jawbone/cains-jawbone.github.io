My guest has, I think, a Byzantine beauty, as of
a golden snake. Is she, or is she not, a little pale
about the Gills? Sanders comes into view
again, seemingly improved by his lunar visit.
He props himself and gazes out to the north-
west over the water of the little bay, drinking
it all in. I follow his gaze and see, as Henry
saw when he was at home in Woodstock,
twisted trees in front of the thick-windowed
little house, and a foreground of exquisitely
coloured vegetation with somewhat the con-
sistency of fur stoles : a breast of the hills under
a long cloud. I have given her nothing at all.
